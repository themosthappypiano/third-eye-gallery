The **Third Eye brand** represents awareness, artistic perception, and individuality.
The logo combines the number **3**, the lettering **rd**, and a stylized **eye symbol** representing insight, creativity, and artistic vision.

The overall brand aesthetic should feel:

Art-forward
Mystical but modern
Minimal
Gallery-oriented
Creative and intentional

The brand should avoid cliché tattoo imagery and instead feel like a **modern art studio identity**.
